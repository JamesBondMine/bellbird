1. Introduction

Welcome to BellBird, a secure and feature-rich communication platform developed by BellBird Development Team. This application enables users to connect through instant messaging, voice calls, video calls, group chats, file sharing, real-time translation, and more. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By using the BellBird application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The BellBird application collects the following types of information:

2.1 Essential Data (Required for App Functionality):

  • Account Information: Username, email address, country/region, profile picture
  • Device Information: IP address, device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Communication Data: Messages, voice calls, video calls, file transfers, and other content you share through the app
  • Usage Logs: Crash reports, feature usage frequency, app performance data

  • Storage Access: For file sharing and media uploads (with your permission)
